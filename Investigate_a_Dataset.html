Dataset Description¶

TMDb movie data This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings and revenue.

column names ['id', 'imdb_id', 'popularity', 'budget', 'revenue', 'original_title', 'cast', 'homepage', 'director', 'tagline', 'keywords', 'overview', 'runtime', 'genres', 'production_companies', 'release_date', 'vote_count', 'vote_average', 'release_year', 'budget_adj', 'revenue_adj'] The final two columns ending with _adj show the budget and revenue of the associated movie in terms of 2010 dollars, accounting for inflation over time.
